Equipment


Mandatory


Helmet: Helmets do not fully protect the player from concussion, but and the same time it protects the player from a potential serious brain injury.

Facemasks: The facemask is the front metal part of the helmet to protect your face from getting the ball or something else to your face.

Shoulder pads: Shoulder pads basically absorbs a lot of the impact and distributes around the area.

Uniforms: Uniforms represent the team you play for and without a uniform it would be confusing because the other team might not know that you are on the other team.

There are many different parts to wear in football but the ones I listed are the main ones you kind of need to wear
